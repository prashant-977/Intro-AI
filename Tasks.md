1.	Quick exercise — rewrite the weak prompt (2 min)
Pair up. One person picks a real business scenario (marketing, HR, finance, ops)
Write the weak version first — one vague sentence
Then add: role + task + format + audience + data placeholder

Share the before/after with the room
2. Closing discussion (3 min) — "Replace or augment?"
•	A marketing analyst used to spend 2 days reading 500 survey responses. Copilot does it in 2 minutes. Is that analyst's job gone — or just changed?
•	If Copilot does the reading, what skill becomes more valuable?
•	How would you verify that Copilot's sentiment summary of your customer data is actually correct?
